## The one you'll hate the most and also the one you'll need the most.
  
[Hacker News] gets roughly 5M views a day. Often we spend much time reading the comments, scrolling. 
So, I decided to build an app that provides you with what you need the most from the HN site, Of Course, the top 10 results.
Besides, the website is totally white and black text, which helps put more strain on your eyes.

I built this minimal website using [Flask] (a lightweight WSGI web application framework) and [the Official HackerNews API] and hosted it on [Heroku], a PaaS that enables developers to build, run, and operate applications entirely in the cloud.
Comic Sans is the most hated font and Yes, I am using it intentionally.
